Job Title: Senior Python Engineer
Location: Austin, TX (Hybrid) or Remote (US-based)

About Us:
At Innovatech Solutions, we're building the next generation of data-driven platforms that revolutionize how businesses operate. We are a fast-paced, collaborative team passionate about clean code, scalable architecture, and solving complex problems. We're looking for an experienced Senior Python Engineer to help lead our backend development efforts and shape the future of our core products.

What You'll Do
As a Senior Python Engineer, you will be a key player in designing, building, and maintaining the robust, scalable backend systems that power our applications. You will not only write high-quality code but also mentor other engineers and drive technical strategy.

Architect & Design: Lead the design and implementation of scalable, secure, and high-performance backend services and APIs using Python.

Develop & Implement: Write clean, maintainable, and well-tested code for new and existing features, primarily using frameworks like Django, Flask, or FastAPI.

Lead & Mentor: Provide technical guidance and mentorship to junior and mid-level engineers, fostering a culture of technical excellence and continuous improvement through code reviews and pair programming.

Collaborate: Work closely with product managers, data scientists, and front-end engineers to translate business requirements into technical solutions.

Optimize & Scale: Identify and resolve performance bottlenecks, and ensure our infrastructure, built on AWS, can handle growing user demand.

Own the Lifecycle: Take ownership of the full development lifecycle, from initial design and prototyping to deployment, monitoring, and maintenance using our CI/CD pipeline.

Who You Are
We're looking for a seasoned engineer with a deep understanding of Python and a passion for building great software.

Required Qualifications:
5+ years of professional software development experience, with a strong focus on Python.

Expert proficiency in Python and its ecosystem.

Extensive experience with web frameworks such as Django, Flask, or FastAPI.

Strong experience designing and building RESTful APIs and microservices.

Proficiency with relational databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL) and/or NoSQL databases (MongoDB, Redis).

Hands-on experience with a major cloud provider (AWS, GCP, or Azure).

Solid understanding of software testing principles and experience with frameworks like pytest.

Proficiency with Git and modern CI/CD practices.
